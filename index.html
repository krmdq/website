me

 
"Consider me a satellite forever orbiting. I knew all the rules, but the rules did not know me" ~ Guaranteed

Lover of beautiful dreams seen with the eyes closed, a dreamer who pursues them with the eyes open. A first-generation entrepreneur exploring the Indian Valley startup ecosystem, dreaming of empowering technology to the masses.

Proponent of creative intelligence and staying original, with an endeavor to inculcate 'A' (art) into 'STEM' (science, technology, engineering and math), as part of being an innovator at dQ.

Strong advocate for KISS (Keep it super simple) methodology in design & simple living, high thinking ideology in life.

Known to Say : "Holy comoly Robin, I forgot to put my Bat detonator in my Bat utility belt. Perhaps iSteve would not mind me using his iPhone to blow up the iVillianPad".

A Lie : After being inspired by the Malayalam thriller ‘Manichitrathazhu’ in his early teens, Krish motivated M Night Shyamalan to deliver an equally brilliant screenplay for Hollywood during his late teens.The result was the widely acclaimed thriller ‘The Sixth Sense’.

Krish currently scripts software products in India, chasing his entrepreneurial dreams.
